Guacamole

Description

Guacamole is a fresh Mexican dip made from mashed avocados, lime juice, and a handful of simple seasonings. It's quick to put together and pairs perfectly with tortilla chips, tacos, or just about anything else.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1/2 small white onion, finely chopped
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 roma tomatoes, diced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced

Steps

  1. Cut the avocados in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a medium bowl.
  2. Mash the avocado with a fork, leaving it a little chunky.
  3. Stir in the chopped onion, lime juice, salt, cumin, and cayenne pepper.
  4. Fold in the cilantro, tomatoes, and minced garlic.
  5. Taste and adjust the salt or lime juice as needed.
  6. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving to let the flavors blend.
  7. Serve with tortilla chips.
